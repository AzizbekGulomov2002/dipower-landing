About the Company

For several years, DIPOWER has been successfully engaged in the supply of mechanical equipment in the Oil and Gas, Chemical, Energy and Manufacturing industries. The main quality of the company is the resources that have been formed with less than 18 years of experience in the markets of Uzbekistan, Turkmenistan, Tajikistan, Mozambique, Dubai, Bahrain, Qatar, Brazil, Argentina, Turkey, the United States and England. The company is rapidly growing and strengthening its mission in the market to achieve maximum record-breaking successful delivery in the shortest possible time.
